SnowNavi Membership Guide

Welcome to SnowNavi Snow Club! Becoming a member means you're not just part of a community of snow enthusiasts, but you also enjoy exclusive benefits, priority course bookings, internal discounts, and event privileges. Here are three ways to obtain membership:

🏂 Join SnowNavi Instructor Team

If you're a snow enthusiast who wants to share your passion and teach others, consider applying to join the SnowNavi instructor team. As an instructor, you'll automatically receive membership, earn money through teaching, participate in internal training, and get priority in course scheduling.

  • ✅ Membership automatically activated
  • 💸 Paid teaching opportunities, instructor development system
  • 📚 Free/discounted instructor advanced training

🎿 Actively Participate in Club Activities

Simply by actively participating in our organized ski activities and meeting certain attendance requirements (see specific activity guidelines for details), you'll automatically be upgraded to membership status and enjoy more skiing benefits.

  • ✅ No additional fees, automatic membership
  • 📅 More participation, more benefits unlocked
  • 🌟 Priority booking for ski lessons and group activities

💳 Pay Annual Fee to Become a Member

You can also become a member by paying a membership fee, valid for one year (from the date of payment).

  • 💶 Annual fee only €50
  • ⏰ One-year validity, join anytime
  • 🎁 Unlock member-exclusive courses, discounts, and benefits

🎁 Exclusive Member Benefits

  • Bataleon - 40% discount on all products
  • Rome SDS - 40% discount on all products
  • Outdoor Master - 40%-50% discount on ski goggles

Important Notes

  • Each member will receive a unique membership number and can use an electronic membership card
  • Instructors and activity participants who meet the criteria will receive membership activation notifications
  • All members can enjoy priority participation in ski activities, course discounts, and internal benefit draws during the validity period
